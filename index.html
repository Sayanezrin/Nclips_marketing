PRIVACY AT NCLIPS.AI
Your data, in plain English.
Last updated · June 2026

nclips.ai is a study platform for nursing students. To run it we collect the minimum data we need to keep your account, your progress, and your subscription working. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. The rest of this page is the detail.

01

What we collect

When you create an account we store your name, email, and a one way hash of your password (we can't read the password itself). If you sign in with Google we store your Google account ID and the email Google gives us. Nothing else.

While you study, we record which videos you watched, how far through each one you got, what you bookmarked, and any chapter progress. None of this is shared with anyone outside the platform.

When you subscribe to a course, Stripe handles your card details on their own servers. We never see or store your full card number.
